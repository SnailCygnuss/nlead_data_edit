Do you want to get funds to develop your research ideas? Take a look at current calls for proposals and start preparing your next proposal Research Calls
Do you want to show the findings from your research and learn what other logistics researchers are doing? Take a look and register for your next conference Conferences
Do you want to know more about current trends in logistics and the latest research? Take a look and register for upcoming seminars Seminars
Do you want to publish your papers in reputed academic journals or see what type of topics are top journals looking for? Take a look at current call for papers and special issues Special Issues
